# WHO Common Cold FAQ (Sample)
**Source:** WHO Public Health Info
**Date:** 2023-10-10

## What is the common cold?
A viral infection of your nose and throat (upper respiratory tract). It's usually harmless, although it might not feel that way.

## Treatment
- Rest and stay hydrated.
- Gargle with salt water for sore throat.
- Honey can help with cough for older children and adults.

## When to see a doctor?
If you have a high fever (above 101F), persistent cough, or difficulty breathing.
