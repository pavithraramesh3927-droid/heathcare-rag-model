# Hypertension Clinical Guideline (Sample)
**Source:** Example Hospital Clinical Docs
**Date:** 2024-05-15
**Standard:** WHO 2023 Guidelines

## Overview
High blood pressure (hypertension) is defined as systolic blood pressure (SBP) >= 140 mmHg and/or diastolic blood pressure (DBP) >= 90 mmHg.

## Symptoms
Often asymptomatic. Severe symptoms include headaches, blurred vision, and chest pain.

## Non-Pharmaceutical Management
- Reduce salt intake (less than 5g per day)
- Increase physical activity (30 mins moderate intensity)
- Weight reduction
- Cessation of tobacco use

## Warning: Heart Attack Symptoms
If a person experiences sudden chest pain radiating to the left arm, shortness of breath, or cold sweats, they may be having a heart attack. Call emergency services immediately.
